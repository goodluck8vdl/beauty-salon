Our hair salon...

is open seven days a week. We provide hair and beauty services which range from hair cutting and styling, to keratin smoothing treatments, to avant-garde hair highlighting techniques such as balayage, to hair extensions and airbrush makeup. We work with the best hair care products and the most innovative hair coloring products. See our services page for available treatments.
Our philosophy is to make sure every customer is beautiful, happy, and satisfied when she leaves our salon. At TriBeCa Beauty Salon we consider our stylists our clients' beauty partners.

Established in 1985, TriBeCa is an independent boutique hair salon that maintains a creative environment with a team of multi-talented, international hairstylists, colorists, makeup artists and photographers. Our team is dedicated to the art of listening; to interpret and craft a look that achieves a mutual appreciation of the beauty that resides within. We measure our success by the level of joy we see in our guests. We are grateful to work with and for all those who walk through our door.
