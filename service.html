Salon Policy

For your convenience, we accept Cash, Debit, Visa, MasterCard, American Express,and Discover credit cards. Prices do not include tax or gratuity. Please be aware that gratuity is cash only and cannot be placed on your card. Please arrive on time to ensure you receive timely service. A credit card is required to hold certain hair appointments and a 24-hour cancellation policy is enforced with these appointments. Failure to adhere to the cancellation policy will result in a charge to the credit card for the full service price.
